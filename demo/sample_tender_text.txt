CENTRAL RESERVE POLICE FORCE
OFFICE OF THE DIG, GROUP CENTRE, BHUBANESWAR
TENDER NOTICE NO: CRPF/GC/BBS/CONST/2025-26/001

NOTICE INVITING TENDER (NIT)
FOR CONSTRUCTION OF TYPE-II BARRACKS AT CRPF CAMP, BHUBANESWAR

1. SCOPE OF WORK:
Construction of Type-II Residential Barracks (Ground + 3 floors) at CRPF Group Centre,
Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The work includes civil construction, electrical works, plumbing,
and allied finishing works as per CPWD specifications.

2. ESTIMATED COST: Rs 6,20,00,000/- (Rupees Six Crore Twenty Lakh Only)
3. EARNEST MONEY DEPOSIT: Rs 12,40,000/- (2% of estimated cost)
4. COMPLETION PERIOD: 18 months from date of award

5. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

5.1 FINANCIAL CRITERIA:

5.1.1 The bidder shall have a minimum average annual turnover of Rs 5,00,00,000/-
(Rupees Five Crore Only) from construction works during the last three financial years
(FY 2022-23, FY 2023-24, FY 2024-25). This is a mandatory requirement. Failure to
meet this criterion shall result in disqualification.
Acceptable documents: CA Certificate, Audited Balance Sheet, ITR.

5.1.2 The bidder must have a minimum net worth of Rs 1,00,00,000/- (Rupees One Crore Only)
as on 31st March 2025. This is essential for financial capability assessment.
Acceptable documents: CA Certificate, Audited Balance Sheet.

5.1.3 The bidder should preferably have a credit facility/solvency certificate for a minimum
of Rs 2,00,00,000/- (Rupees Two Crore Only) from a scheduled bank.
Acceptable documents: Bank Solvency Certificate.

5.2 TECHNICAL CRITERIA:

5.2.1 The bidder shall have successfully completed at least 3 (three) similar construction
works (RCC frame construction, minimum G+2 structure) each costing not less than
Rs 2,00,00,000/- during the last 7 (seven) years ending on the date of submission.
Acceptable documents: Completion Certificate, Work Order, Experience Letter.

5.2.2 The bidder must have RCC frame construction experience with post-tensioned slabs
in seismic zone III between 2019 and 2022. This specialized experience is mandatory
for the structural requirements of this project.
Acceptable documents: Completion Certificate from client, Structural Engineer Certificate.

5.2.3 The bidder should have an in-house team of minimum 5 qualified engineers
(B.E./B.Tech Civil) with at least 3 years of experience each.
Acceptable documents: HR records, Engineer qualification certificates.

5.3 COMPLIANCE CRITERIA:

5.3.1 The bidder must have valid GST Registration. This is mandatory.
Acceptable documents: GST Registration Certificate.

5.3.2 The bidder shall possess valid PAN Card.
Acceptable documents: PAN Card copy.

5.3.3 The bidder must have ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System certification
from an accredited certification body. This is a mandatory requirement.
Acceptable documents: ISO Certificate.

5.3.4 The bidder should preferably have ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management)
certification. This is desirable but not mandatory.
Acceptable documents: ISO 14001 Certificate.

5.3.5 The bidder must not be blacklisted by any Central/State Government department
or PSU as on the date of submission of bid. A self-declaration on company letterhead
is required.

6. BID SUBMISSION:
All bids must be submitted through the CPPP portal (eprocure.gov.in) before
17:00 hours on 15th June 2025.

7. BID OPENING:
Technical bids will be opened on 16th June 2025 at 11:00 hours.
Financial bids of technically qualified bidders will be opened on 20th June 2025.

8. GENERAL CONDITIONS:
All terms and conditions as per GFR 2017 and CPWD Manual shall apply.
